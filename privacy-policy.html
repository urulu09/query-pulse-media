Privacy Policy — Query Pulse Bot

Last updated: 2026-07-16

Query Pulse Bot is a personal automation tool used solely by its owner to publish original, AI-assisted educational video content (about SQL and DevOps topics) to the owner's own social media accounts, including TikTok (@query.pulse).

Data we access

The app uses TikTok's Content Posting API only to upload video content to the owner's own TikTok account. It does not access, collect, store, or share any data belonging to other TikTok users. No personal data is collected from viewers or third parties.

Data we store

The app stores only what is required to operate: OAuth access/refresh tokens for the owner's own account (used solely to authenticate API requests) and a log of previously published video topics (to avoid duplicate content). No user-identifiable data beyond the owner's own account is retained.
